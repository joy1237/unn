4. Bananas


Peel Back the Benefits: Why Bananas Are a Superfood

Due to their rich nutrient profile, they are a good source of potassium, fiberand vitamin C which provide energy and improve digestion Key benefits include:

  1. Bananas are good energy booster. They are a popular choice for athletes seeking a pre- or post-workout boost.
  2. The fiber in Bananas, both soluble and insoluble, helps to prevent constipation.
  3. They also can help maintain healthy blood presure and fluid balance.
  4. May support mood regulation.
