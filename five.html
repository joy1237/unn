5. Carrots


Rooted in Health: The Importance of Carrots.

Carrots are well known for Supporting eye health, particularly night vision due to their high levels of vitamin A and bete-carotene. Some key benefits include:

  1. Essential for maintaining healthy vision.
  2. May help reduce risk of stroke and heart attack
  3. Support healthy liver function
  4. The antioxidants in carrots may help protect against cognitive decline and improve brain function.
